About Us

ID Interior Design was founded in 2019 when the company's CEO, Irina Dragomirescu, back then an aspiring designer with an antreprenorial spirit, gathered a team of designers and business specialists and created ID. Since then, the company has made its goal to bring its clients dreams to reality, to warm hearts and transform apartments into homes.

We are committed to finding the best solution and design for your home with our team of experts and professionals. Your wishes are our priority and we will always do our best to fullfill them.
